VerinLegno S.p.A.

VerinLegno S.p.A. is an Italian company founded in Massa e Cozzile in 1975. The company develops high-technology, reliable and increasingly green coatings for wood, metal, glass, interiors and exteriors. With over 40 years of experience, VerinLegno has distribution in many countries and offers a wide catalogue of products.
